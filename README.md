# 🚀 FlowAI GTM Engine

I designed the Go-To-Market (GTM) foundation for **FlowAI**, a fictional AI Meeting Assistant startup.
---

## 🎯 Project Objective

Design and build the foundational GTM system for FlowAI by answering key business questions and implementing the initial sales workflow.

---

- ✅ Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- ✅ Buyer Personas

- ✅ Value Proposition

# 🏢 About FlowAI

FlowAI is a fictional SaaS startup that helps sales teams automatically capture meeting notes, action items, and follow-ups using AI.

---

# 👥 Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Industry
- B2B SaaS

## Company Size
- 50–500 Employees

## Target Teams
- Sales Teams
- Customer Success
- Revenue Operations

## Challenges
- Too many customer meetings
- Manual note-taking
- Missed follow-ups
- Scattered meeting information

---

# 👤 Buyer Persona

## Primary Persona

**Head of Sales**

Responsible for:
- Sales productivity
- Pipeline performance
- Team efficiency

### Pain Points
- Reps spend too much time writing notes.
- Follow-ups are inconsistent.
- Important customer information gets lost.

---

## Secondary Personas

- Founder
- Revenue Operations Manager

---

# 💥 Customer Pain Points

- Manual meeting notes
- Missed action items
- Poor follow-up process
- Information scattered across multiple tools
- Time wasted updating CRMs
