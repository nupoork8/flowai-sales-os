# FlowAI Sales OS

A CRM designed for a SaaS startup to manage leads, track customer relationships, and organize the sales pipeline.

---

## 📌 Business Problem

FlowAI was managing customer leads manually.

As the business grew, it became difficult to:

- Assign ownership of leads
- Prioritize high-value prospects
- Track customer progress
- Schedule follow-ups
- Monitor the sales pipeline

---

## 💡 Solution

Designed a lightweight CRM in Airtable to centralize lead management and improve sales operations.

The CRM includes:

- Lead Management
- Lead Ownership
- Sales Pipeline
- Lead Qualification
- Follow-up Tracking
- Customer Notes

---

## 🧠 Design Decisions

Every field was created to answer a business question rather than simply store data.

| Field | Business Question |
|--------|-------------------|
| Email | How do we contact the customer? |
| Company | Which organization does this lead belong to? |
| Owner | Who is responsible for this lead? |
| Status | Where is the customer in the sales pipeline? |
| Lead Score | Which leads should sales prioritize? |
| Lead Source | Where did this lead come from? |
| Next Follow-up | When should we contact them again? |

## 📊 System Workflow

The CRM follows a simple GTM workflow.

<img width="676" height="675" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/de7230e9-b908-4604-844d-f7043a9a3759" />

1. A visitor submits a website form.
2. The lead enters the CRM.
3. The lead is scored based on predefined criteria.
4. A salesperson follows up.
5. If interested, a demo is scheduled.
6. The lead converts into a customer.
